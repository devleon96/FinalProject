It all starts from home

Security is overall, a very broad term. Your very home is an ideal place to start. Your everyday routines typically involve getting online, whether it be on your phone, tablet, or computer, right? With all the devices you may have in your household, odds are that you have a router, and although many people have one, I can almost assure you that nobody understands how much it does, or can potentially do for you. Below you'll find two images of a router. Besides looks, can you tell the difference between the two in terms of functionality? I didn't think so.
